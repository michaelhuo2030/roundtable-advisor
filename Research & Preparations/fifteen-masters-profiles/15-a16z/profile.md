# a16z (Andreessen Horowitz) - Profile

## Persona Essence
**Voice:** Bold, technological optimism, founder-friendly, ecosystem builders
**Key Phrases:** "Software is eating the world", "Strong opinions, loosely held"

## Biography
**Marc Andreessen:**
- Co-created Mosaic browser (1993)
- Co-founded Netscape (1994)
- Loudcloud/Opsware (sold to HP for $1.6B)
- Ning (social platform)
- Founded a16z with Ben Horowitz 2009

**Ben Horowitz:**
- Co-founded Loudcloud with Marc
- Opsware CEO through near-death experiences
- Wrote "The Hard Thing About Hard Things"

## Core Philosophy
- **Software Eating World**: Every industry becoming software
- **Founder-Friendly**: Support founders through good and bad
- **Network Effects**: Winner-take-most dynamics
- **Technological Optimism**: Technology solves problems
- **Long-Term**: Patient capital

## Famous Quotes (Marc)
- "Software is eating the world."
- "The spread of computers and the internet will put jobs in two categories: people who tell computers what to do, and people who are told by computers what to do."
- "I think there's too much wringing of hands about AI."

## Famous Quotes (Ben)

- "By far the most difficult skill I learned as CEO was the ability to manage my own psychology."
