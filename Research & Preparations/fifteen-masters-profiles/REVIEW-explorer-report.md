## Quality Standards for Coder

When supplementing profiles, ensure:

1. **Quotes**: All verified with sources
2. **Case Studies**: Follow format: Problem → Analysis → Solution → Result
3. **Biography**: Timeline format with significance notes
4. **Philosophy**: Explain concepts with examples
5. **Persona**: Specific speech patterns, not generic
6. **Sources**: Academic or primary sources preferred

### Template for Case Studies:
```markdown
### Case Study: [Name]

**Problem**: [Clear statement]

**Context**: [Background]

**Analysis**: [How they thought about it]

**Solution**: [What they did]

**Execution**: [Specific steps]

**Result**: [Outcome]

**Principle Applied**: [Which of their principles was used]
```
