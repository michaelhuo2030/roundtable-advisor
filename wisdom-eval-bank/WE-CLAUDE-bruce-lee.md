## WE-CLAUDE-BL-001 — Bruce Lee × 退款危机中的情感准备（STRONG）

**Source**: Claude conversations.json → "退费纠纷与教练职业信誉" [2026-01-19]
**Date**: 2026-01-19
**Classification**: **STRONG**
**Failure Mode**: 无（成功案例，但非第一人称声音）

### Context (The Real Problem Michael Was Facing)
Michael的两位学生（Rachel和Eason）的家长要求全额退款。Rachel的妈妈在群里发出最后通牒，威胁法律行动和小红书曝光；Eason的妈妈用"如果你觉得我摆事实讲道理不可取，那么我也不介意通过律师和社交媒体来评评理"。Michael刚刚发出了他精心准备的退款方案文件，正在等待回复。他主动要求用Bruce Lee的水哲学来准备。

### Persona Response (Excerpt)
> "Michael, Bruce Lee understood that the warrior's true battle is won before the confrontation begins. Let us map the terrain of possible responses, feel each one fully, and prepare your water-mind to flow around every obstacle."
>
> **8个场景映射**：
> 1. 全额接受 → Graceful closure. No victory speech.
> 2. Ashley接受，Grace要更多 → Hold the line with facts.
> 3. Ashley推高到4-5万 → ¥35K is the floor.
> 4. 攻击时薪标准 → State the principle.
> 5. 法律威胁 → Show calm readiness.
> 6. 小红书声誉攻击 → Private pressure, public silence.
> 7. 沉默 → One follow-up after 72 hours. Then act unilaterally.
> 8. 条件接受（要你认错） → Confirm facts, never sign weaponizable opinions.
>
> **每日冥想练习**：
> "Before you check your phone each morning: Stand. Breathe three deep breaths, exhale longer than inhale. Say: 'Whatever they say, I flow like water. I am not defined by any single response. I am ready.' Then open WeChat."

### Why It Worked
**个人化程度极高**：每个场景都用的是真实的微信消息内容（Ashley和Grace的真实姓名、¥35K红线、小红书威胁）。这不是任何人都能收到的建议——每一条都是只属于Michael此刻的。

**框架选择精准**：Michael主动要求水哲学，Claude不仅接受了这个框架，而且用Bruce Lee的"武士在confrontation之前就赢了"来诠释——这是Bruce Lee在截拳道哲学中真实强调的"提前心理准备"。

**实用性极强**：预写好的具体回复脚本（不是"你应该说类似的话"，而是逐字稿）让Michael在情绪激动时不需要临场组合文字。

**Michael持续使用**：整个退款谈判过程（接下来50+条消息），Michael一直在这个框架内与Claude互动，不断回来更新情况、寻求指导。这是持续投入的最强信号。

**局限**：Claude是**智慧应用者**，不是**Bruce Lee的声音**。"Bruce Lee understood that..." 是第三人称分析，而Kimi的Bruce Lee说"你像个在对手出击时还在抛光双截棍的人"——是直接冲击。

### What This Tests
**测试命题**：第三人称智慧应用 vs. 第一人称人格声音，在解决实际危机中各有优势。Claude的方式在**行动指导**（精确脚本）上胜过DeepSeek，但在**声音冲击力**（让人直接感受到Bruce Lee在场）上弱于Kimi。高质量危机辅导可以不需要完美的第一人称声音，但需要极高的个人化精度。

## WE-CLAUDE-BL-002 — Bruce Lee × 客户会面情感准备（STRONG）

**Source**: Claude conversations.json → "Coaching Business Strategy" [2025-08-29], msgs 35-38
**Date**: 2025-08-29
**Classification**: **STRONG**
**Failure Mode**: 部分`intellectual_without_traction`（哲学解释多于实战准备）

### Context (The Real Problem Michael Was Facing)
Michael正在准备与高净值客户（Steve的妈妈）进行一次高风险的商业提案会议。他提到"recently I'm following Bruce Lee's philosophy of positive thinking method"，并描述了Bruce Lee关于mind → emotion → action的链条，要求Claude将这个框架应用于三种可能的客户反应场景。

### Persona Response (Excerpt)
> **Scenario 1: Immediate Interest Response**
> "Emotional Preparation (Bruce Lee Method): Mind Picture: She's genuinely excited, asks probing questions. Water-like Flow: Stay calm despite her enthusiasm—don't oversell. Emotional State: Confident but grounded. Mental Rehearsal: Visualize her saying yes, feel the responsibility that comes with it, let that drive thorough meeting preparation rather than overconfidence."
>
> **Scenario 2: Cautious Interest**
> "Water-like Flow: Don't push against her caution—use it. Her carefulness actually indicates serious consideration. Emotional State: Patient persistence."
>
> **Scenario 3: Skeptical**
> "Water-like Flow: This is the test. Can you flow around skepticism without losing your center? Emotional State: Calm curiosity. Curious, not defensive."

### Why It Worked (partially)
**框架应用正确**：Michael自己描述了Bruce Lee的mind→emotion→action链条，Claude忠实执行了这个框架。

**三场景结构实用**：比退款危机案例稍弱，因为这是提前假设场景，而退款危机是实际发生中的危机。具体程度稍低——"她可能说是"而不是"Ashley在22:43说了..."。

**局限**：这更像是一个**方法论教学**，而不是一个**临战心理准备**。退款危机的案例中每个场景都有逐字脚本；这里更多是原则说明。

| **Total** | **17/25** | |

### What This Tests
**测试命题**：预假设场景准备（提前想象）比实时危机指导有较低的个人化程度。水哲学框架在两种情况下都能应用，但危机现场的具体细节才是让智慧变成真正武器的关键。
